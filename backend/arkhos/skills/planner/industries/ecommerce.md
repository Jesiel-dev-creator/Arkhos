# E-commerce Landing Pages

## Section Order

Hero (featured product or collection + headline + shop CTA) → Trust Badges (secure payment, free shipping, returns policy) → Featured Products (4-8 product cards with images, prices, ratings) → Categories (visual grid of product categories) → Value Propositions (3-4 reasons to buy: quality, price, shipping, support) → Customer Reviews (with photos and star ratings) → Newsletter Signup (discount incentive) → FAQ (shipping, returns, sizing) → Footer (payment methods, policies, contact).

## Copy Patterns

E-commerce copy is action-oriented and trust-building. Product headlines focus on the benefit or lifestyle, not just the product name: "Everyday comfort, elevated" not "Cotton T-Shirt Collection". Pricing should always be visible — hiding prices loses trust. Use urgency sparingly and honestly: "Limited edition" only for genuinely limited items. Free shipping thresholds are powerful: "Free shipping on orders over EUR 50". Review quotes should mention specific product qualities.

## Key Features to Include

- Product cards with image, name, price, and rating stars
- Add-to-cart or shop-now CTAs on every product card
- Trust badges: SSL secure, free returns, fast shipping
- Payment method logos (Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, Apple Pay)
- Size guide or product specification links
- Newsletter signup with first-order discount

## Design Aesthetic

Clean and product-focused. White or light backgrounds to let product photography shine. Minimal color palette with one accent for CTAs (often black, coral, or brand-specific). Grid-based product layouts with consistent card sizes. High-quality product photography on white or lifestyle backgrounds. Sans-serif fonts throughout for clarity (DM Sans, Outfit, Manrope). Subtle hover effects on product cards (shadow lift, image zoom). Mobile-first layout is critical — most e-commerce traffic is mobile.

## Photo Categories

Product photography on clean backgrounds, lifestyle shots showing products in use, flat-lay compositions, unboxing moments, detail close-ups showing material quality. Consistent lighting and styling across all product images is essential for a professional appearance.
